# Design Direction A — "Precision Dark"

> Saved for future reference. Not implemented. May revisit.

## Concept
Near-black base with a single warm amber accent. Think Oxide Computer meets Linear.
The amber reads as circuitboard gold — nothing in devtools uses it, making it
immediately distinctive. No neon. No gradients. Typography does all the heavy lifting.

## Palette
- Background: #0C0E13
- Surface: #13151C
- Border: rgba(255,255,255,0.07)
- Primary accent: #D4A853 (warm amber / circuitboard gold)
- Text primary: #E8E6E1 (warm off-white)
- Text muted: #6B7280

## Typography
- Headline: 80–96px, Inter 600, tight tracking -0.03em
- No decorative fonts — system hierarchy only
- Amber accent used sparingly in headlines for emphasis

## Animations
- Restraint-first: elements fade + slide 24px into place
- Nothing bounces
- Scroll reveal only, no hover theatrics

## Layout
- Aggressive negative space
- Max-width 1100px centered
- 120px vertical section padding

## Personality
- Dead serious, senior-engineer energy
- Authority through precision, not decoration
- The canvas product screenshot is the hero visual
- Amber used only for: primary CTA, step numbers, key callouts

## Why It Was Set Aside
Direction C (Monochrome + One) was chosen for its more dramatic
scroll animations and sage green accent which feels fresher.
Direction A remains strong for a future enterprise-facing iteration.
